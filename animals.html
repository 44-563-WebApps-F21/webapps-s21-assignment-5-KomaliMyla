Monkey

Monkey is a common name that may refer to most mammals of the infraorder Simiiformes, also known as the simians.

Kangaroo

Kangaroos have large, powerful hind legs, large feet adapted for leaping, a long muscular tail for balance, and a small head. Like most marsupials.

Lion

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large felid of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, deep-chested body, short, rounded head, round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
